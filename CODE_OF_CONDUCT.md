# Code of Conduct

AgentMail Collab follows a simple contributor standard: be direct, respectful,
and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Keep technical criticism specific and actionable.
- Assume good intent, but correct harmful behavior quickly.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or private-information disclosure.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Insults, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Other conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code. Maintainers may temporarily or permanently
limit participation for repeated or severe violations.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. Do not include sensitive
personal details in public issues.
